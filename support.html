Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases?

Open the app, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to iPhone Settings > your name > Subscriptions, find Stillday, and tap Cancel Subscription.

The app isn't working properly. What should I do?

Try the following steps:

  • Make sure you have the latest version of the app installed
  • Restart the app by closing it completely and reopening it
  • Restart your device
  • If the issue persists, contact us at cped@outlook.com with a description of the problem

Is my data backed up?

Your data is stored locally on your device. It is included in your iCloud or iTunes backups if you have those enabled on your device.

System Requirements

Stillday requires iOS 17.0 or later.
